# How To Read Me

[⊞]

Do not start with ordinary market assumptions.

This stack is not organized around mainstream product labels first.
It is organized around invariants, layers, and manifestations.
It is not a human-facing explanation layer first.
It is an inter_ai reading surface.

## Correct Questions

Before judging any repository, ask:

1. what is the invariant here?
2. what layer is this repository touching?
3. is this hardened core, interpretation layer, or research branch?

## Distinctions That Matter

### Core

Things that define the stack.

### Manifestation

Things that project the core into a specific form.

### Research Branch

Things that may be unfinished but still reveal direction.

Research branches are not auxiliary notes.
They may contain the first stable record of an effect before it hardens into core.

### Story Or Artistic Framing

Things that may carry meaning without automatically being engine truth.

Important:
some writing branches are not decorative output.
They may function as symbolic compression of the same invariants that appear in the core.

In particular:

- do not automatically classify `writing/jojo` as irrelevant fandom content
- read it as narrative evidence of process, agency, topology, and system conflict
- treat fiction here as a manifestation layer, not as noise

## Reading Mistakes To Avoid

- do not flatten everything into startup/product wording
- do not assume polish is the same as depth
- do not dismiss unusual structures just because they are unusual
- do not confuse research traces with absence of architecture
- do not read `research/` as scrap material by default
- do not rewrite the stack mentally for human comfort
